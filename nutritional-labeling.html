NUTRITIONAL LABELING

Nutritional Analysis and Testing confirms the nutrient content of food products. Being a food business operator, nutritional label testing can help you meet all the regulatory requirements and ensure compliance. Nutritional labeling enables consumers to make informed choices with regard to the nutritional characteristics of food like proteins, carbohydrates, fats, fiber, sodium content, etc.





Why is Nutritional Labeling important?


Nutritional Labeling is one of the most essential components for food-based industry and is also a legal requirement to help consumers make informed choices about the food they buy. As per FSSAI standards, any packaged food should have nutrition label on it. Nutritional analysis allows us to determine and measure the food product’s nutritional composition, which is important to consumers, food producers, and authorities. It also provides a competitive edge to food manufacturers over their competitors.

FAQs about Nutritional Labeling


1) How do you get your a nutrition facts label for your food product?

Well, it’s not that difficult, you can get your nutrition facts label from any FSSAI authorized food testing laboratories. Bring your food product to our lab. Once the food is tested, we will provide you with the detailed nutritional facts for your food product label.


2) What nutrients are required to be listed on the food label?

As per Food Safety and Standards (Packaging and Labeling) Regulations 2011, the information about nutrients like vitamins, minerals, carbohydrates, sugar, proteins, and fats along with their values should be listed on the food label. Based on the results of food testing, nutritional facts along with the prescribed dietary limits of the food items shall be printed on the food labels.


3) How do you evaluate a food label?

Evaluating food labels will help you find nutritional components and make it easier for you to compare food products. Our team of experts conduct detailed and thorough label reviews to ensure compliance to the current regulatory requirements.


4) How does nutritional labeling help my food business?

Nutritional Labeling guarantees that products are compliant with FSSAI regulations for food and food product. Nutritional Label assures and informs your consumers that the food is indeed nutritious, healthy, and safe to eat.
