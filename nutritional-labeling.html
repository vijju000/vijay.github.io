NUTRITIONAL LABELING

Nutritional Labeling identify the number of ingredients and other components present in the concerned product. FSSAI mandates the presence of all its ingredients and composition mentioned on the product’s packaging label as per Food Safety and Standards (Labelling and Packaging) Regulations, 2011.





Benefits


– Helps maintain Transparency among consumers
– Facilitates the choice of ‘Informed Purchase’
– Helps comply with Food Safety Regulations
– Provides complete information about the Product
– Guarantees safety and security of the product and the brand
– Mentioning of Allergenic product helps prevent health hazards
– Helps Food Business identify the further aspects of Label Validation, Sensory Analysis, etc

Different products that need Nutritional Labeling :


 - Namkeen  - Chips
 - Ready to eat foods  - Cooked foods
 - Bakery products  - Dairy products
 - Meat and meat products  - Health drinks
 - Dietary supplements  - Protein Powders
 - Health drinks  - Infant formula
 - Biscuits and cookies  - Candies and sweets
