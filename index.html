Why is Food Testing Important?

Food testing is essential as we know that contaminated food can lead to food poisoning and to ensure food safety, the analysis of food is extremely important. It follows that the food we consume should be safe in all aspects and should not harm the body. For this reason, it is of paramount importance that the food production process should have a rigorous monitoring and testing component built into it.
